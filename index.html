Why Pinpoint?

⚡

Native Performance

Built on the Windows Magnification API for GPU-accelerated, lag-free zoom. No slow screenshot loops.

🎮

Gaming Ready

Click-through overlay means your mouse still works in-game. Toggle with any key or mouse button.

🔍

Two Zoom Modes

Follow your mouse cursor in real time, or lock the scope to the center of any monitor you choose.

💻

Multi-Monitor Support

Detects all connected displays. Pick which monitor to magnify — perfect for multi-screen setups.

⚙️

Fully Customizable

Adjust zoom (1x-5x), scope size (100-600px), trigger key, refresh rate, crosshair, and behavior on the fly.

📁

Persistent Settings

Every change auto-saves to a JSON file. Reopen Pinpoint and your preferences are right where you left them.

💡

Zero Dependencies

Only needs Python and Pillow. No heavy frameworks or complex installers.

🔓

Open Source

MIT licensed. Free to use, modify, and share. Community contributions welcome.

Built For

🎮

Gamers

Zoom in on distant targets, read small UI text, inspect fine details without leaning in.

🎨

Designers

Pixel-perfect editing, color inspection, and detail work without eye strain.

🖨️

Creators

Stream zoomed-in highlights to your audience for tutorials and gameplay moments.

♿

Accessibility

Quick magnification wherever your cursor goes, without disrupting your workflow.
